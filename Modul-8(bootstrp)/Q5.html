Q1. What do you know about bootstrep grid system?

Ans.
  • Bootstrep is easy to use and fully responsive so it can easily adject with all screen size.
  • There three majaor components in bootstrep grid continer, row, column.
  • There are 12 columns in one row. if you do not want all 12 colums than you can grouped it as per screen.
  • bootstrep grid system have classes for column as per screen size
    • col-xs: for phones - screen less than 768px.
    • col-sm: for tablets - screen equals to or grather than 768px.
    • col-md: for small laptops - screen equals to or grather than 992px.
    • col-lg: for laptops - screen equals to or grather than 1200px.
    • col-xl and col-xxl is use for big screen.
